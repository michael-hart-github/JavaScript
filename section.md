The `section` element i used to divide parts of HTML into different sections.

The `section` element does have a [[semantic]] meaning.

# Example of section

```HTML
<section>
  <h2>Mammals</h2>
  <p>
    Mammals are warm-blooded animals with fur or hair. Most give birth to live
    young.
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>Lion</li>
    <li>Elephant</li>
    <li>Dolphin</li>
  </ul>
</section>
```
